How we verify

Hotels are discovered from OpenStreetMap. The family-fit score (0–100) is computed by reading each hotel's official website with a multilingual rule-based recognizer: a family service counts only if backed by the original sentence quoted from the site, re-verified word for word.

Nothing is invented. A missing service in our data doesn't mean the hotel lacks it — only that we didn't find it stated on the site. We publish a page only when there is enough proof.

The score is our own proprietary assessment, not an average of reviews.
